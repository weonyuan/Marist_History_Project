Professor Daniel Kirk, former Chairman of the Psychology Department, built the Kirk House in 1976 for his private residence. Dr. Kirk founded the undergraduate Psychology Department in 1962 and the first graduate program at Marist College in 1972. He also developed the first Five-Year Combined BA/MA Program in Psychology in the nation that now serves as a model for similar programs at the College and elsewhere. Dr. Kirk generously willed his home to Marist College.
